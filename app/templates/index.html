How it works

1

You click "Login"

Your app redirects you to the provider (GitHub, Google, etc.)

2

You authorize

The provider asks: "Allow this app to see your profile?" You click yes.

3

Code → Token

The provider sends back a code. Your app exchanges it for an access token.

4

Fetch your data

Your app uses the token to call the API and get your profile info.

💡 Tip: Keep your terminal open while you login. Every step of the OAuth flow is logged with clear labels so you can see exactly what's happening behind the scenes.
